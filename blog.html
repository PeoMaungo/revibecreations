Candle Making

Step 1: Filter used cooking oil to remove particles.
Step 2: Prepare jars and place wick in center.
Step 3: Heat oil using double boiler method.
Step 4: Add natural fragrance oils & colors.
Step 5: Pour into containers and cool.

Plastic Product Making

Step 1: Collect plastic waste from communities.
Step 2: Sort plastics by color and type.
Step 3: Wash and dry thoroughly.
Step 4: Shred into tiny flakes.
Step 5: Melt and mold into eco-products.
